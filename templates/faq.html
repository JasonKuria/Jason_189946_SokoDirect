FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and selling on SokoDirect.

For Buyers
Browse products, click "Add to Cart", then go to your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout". Fill in your delivery address, choose M-Pesa as your payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive an STK push on your phone to enter your M-Pesa PIN.
You can browse and add items to your cart as a guest. However, you'll need to create a free account to complete checkout and track your orders. Registration takes less than 2 minutes!

For Farmers
Create a free account, complete your farmer profile, then click "Post an Item / Produce AD" in the navigation bar. Fill in your product details, upload a clear photo, set your price and available quantity, and your listing goes live immediately.
Listing your produce on SokoDirect is completely free. We only charge a small commission on successful sales to keep the platform running and improving. No upfront fees, no monthly charges.
Payments are processed via M-Pesa and settled to your registered M-Pesa number within 24-48 hours of a confirmed delivery. You can track all your earnings and payouts from your Farmers Dashboard.
Currently, farmers coordinate delivery with buyers directly. Buyers can contact you via the messaging system, phone, or WhatsApp to arrange logistics. SokoDirect is building a dedicated delivery network which will be available soon.
Payments & Pricing
Currently we support Lipa na M-Pesa (STK Push). Bank card payments via Visa and Mastercard are coming soon. We do not accept cash on delivery for security reasons.
Yes. All M-Pesa transactions are processed through Safaricom's secure STK Push system. SokoDirect never stores your PIN or financial credentials. We will never ask you for your PIN, CVV, or password via phone or email.
Refunds are handled case by case. If your order is not fulfilled or arrives in unsatisfactory condition, contact support within 24 hours. Approved refunds are processed back to your M-Pesa within 3-5 business days.
Delivery & Logistics
Delivery times depend on the farmer's location and your county. Within Nairobi, same-day or next-day delivery is common. Upcountry deliveries typically take 1-3 business days. The farmer will confirm an estimated time after your order is placed.
SokoDirect has farmers and buyers across all 47 counties. Delivery coverage depends on the specific farmer — some deliver locally only while others ship nationwide. Check the product listing or message the farmer to confirm delivery to your area.
Delivery fees start from KES 150 for pick-up station collection and from KES 350 for doorstep delivery. The exact fee is calculated at checkout based on your location and the farmer's location.
